How to Calculate Percentages

A percentage is a way to express a number as a fraction of 100. The word "percent" comes from the Latin "per centum" meaning "per hundred."

Use our calculator for instant results, or learn the formulas to calculate mentally!

Common Percentage Uses

  • Shopping: Calculate discounts and sale prices
  • Finance: Interest rates, tax calculations, tips
  • School: Grade scores and test results
  • Business: Profit margins, growth rates, commissions
  • Health: Body fat percentage, nutrition labels

Percentage Tips & Tricks

  • 10% trick: Move decimal one place left (10% of 85 = 8.5)
  • 1% trick: Move decimal two places left (1% of 300 = 3)
  • 50%: Just divide by 2
  • 25%: Divide by 4
  • 5%: Find 10%, then halve it
